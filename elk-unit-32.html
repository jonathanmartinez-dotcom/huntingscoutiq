The country

Terrain ranges from rolling sagebrush to steep-sided rugged canyons, especially in the south; pinyon-juniper and sage low give way to sage with aspen stands and some dark timber up high.

Access and pressure

Largely BLM with the best hunting in remote areas reached by foot, horseback or ATV; rain brings muddy roads and high snow closes upper roads.

How to hunt Unit 32

Push into the remote high-elevation pockets of green timber and aspen where elk hold, and watch the pinyon-juniper expanses that pressure pushes bulls into.
