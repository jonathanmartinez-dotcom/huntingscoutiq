Our story

Built by two hunters tired of guessing.

We built ScoutIQ because we are hunters who were tired of guessing. Old pins, forum posts, weather apps, none of it added up to a decision. We wanted one system that told us where to start and why.

We tested it in the field last weekend and it put us on two herds. But it is early. It will not always be right, and some things are still rough around the edges.

Hunting is what we love, and that is the whole reason this exists. We are not charging a dime during the beta. We will keep making it better on the regular. Tell us what the map gets right and wrong. That is how this gets good for all of us.

Jake & Jon · ScoutIQ
What we are building

A wildlife intelligence platform

Not another map or weather app. ScoutIQ forecasts where animals move and guides you to the highest-probability country. Colorado elk first. More states and species as the model learns.
